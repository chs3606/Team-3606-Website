Team 3606 Sponsors

Team 3606 is sponsored by Catholic High School in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

Bear Metal Team 3606 is proud to call Catholic High School home. Founded in 1894 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, CHS has built over 130 years of academic and athletic excellence. The only school in the nation to be recognized six times as a National Blue Ribbon School of Excellence by the U.S. Department of Education, Catholic High is more than a school — it's a legacy. With over 113 state championships across multiple sports and a community that pushes students to be their best, it's no surprise that a team like Bear Metal was born here. Thank you to Catholic High School for giving us the space, the support, and the spirit to compete. Learn more at catholichigh.org.
