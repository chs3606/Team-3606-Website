This Year

Team 3606 is competing in the Magnolia Regional in Laurel, Mississippi. The Magnolia Regional is being held Wednesday, March 18 to Saturday, March 21, 2026. There are 46 teams in the Magnolia Regional with a list of those teams found at FIRST's website. This year, teams have six weeks to design, build, and program their robot to compete in this year's competition, with the top placers earning a spot at the FIRST World Championship.

In REBUILT™ presented by Haas, FIRST® Robotics Competition teams will use engineering skills and re-imagine the past. Every artifact we uncover holds a story. Each tool, each innovation, each work of art connects us to the people and ideas that came before us. Using STEM skills and teamwork, today we can dig deeper into discoveries than ever before.

This year, Bear Metal Team 3606 is running a custom swerve drive base, giving us greater maneuverability and precision on the field compared to traditional drive systems. For our intake, we're using the kitbot — a reliable and proven solution that lets us focus our energy on other areas of the robot.
